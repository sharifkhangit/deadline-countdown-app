🧡 How It Works

  1. The landing page introduces the project and links to the countdown feature.
  2. The countdown component calls /api/deadline (mocked by the interceptor).
  3. The response gives secondsLeft which initializes a timer.
  4. The timer decrements every second until the deadline is reached.
